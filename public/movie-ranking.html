The Best movies according to Aman

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1.) Queen

Female Centric, a homely girl goes to Paris for her solo honeymoon after her Fiance calls off their wedding.

2.) Tanu Weds Manu Returns

Quirky Romcom with a double role by my favourite bollywood actress.

3.) Gangs of Wasseypur

A gritty movie about inter-generational rural gangs and rivalry
